Amadeus · Case study

Problem

Legacy booking core was blocking every new platform feature and couldn't scale with travel demand recovery.

Approach

Sequenced a zero-downtime migration in reversible slices instead of a single cutover, with a live rollback path at each stage.

Trade-off

Chose a slower phased rollout over a faster big-bang launch, trading speed for the ability to walk back any single stage.

Outcome

Business line now on modern platform, unblocking roadmap velocity.
